# Redefining AI Oversight
Artificial Intelligence isn't just a technical shift; it's a behavioral one. In this project, I explore how we can govern AI systems by understanding the mental processes of the users interacting with them.

## My Approach

* **Culture** Measured Impact: This focuses on reducing ungoverned use cases by 50% through psychological nudges rather than rigid blocks.
